6th Annual Knights of Columbus Charity Golf Outing

Saturday, October 10, 2026 • The Club at Gateway — Fort Myers, FL

The Knights of Columbus St. John XXIII invite you to join us for our 6th Annual Charity Golf Outing. Each year, this event brings together golfers, sponsors, parishioners, and community members for a wonderful day of fellowship, friendly competition, and charitable impact.

Thanks to the generosity of our participants and sponsors, the 2025 outing raised nearly $15,000 for local charities. We extend our sincere gratitude to all who participated, volunteered, or sponsored past events—your support makes a real difference in our community.

Whether you’re interested in playing, sponsoring, or volunteering for the 2026 outing, we’d love to keep you informed and involved.
