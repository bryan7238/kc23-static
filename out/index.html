Members-Only Section (access restricted)

To obtain access:

  1. You need a Google account to obtain access to the Members Only section of the website. This can be a Gmail address. Or you can set up a Google account using a non-gmail email address at accounts.google.com/SignUpWithoutGmail
  2. Once you have a Google account, you may use the feedback link to request access to the members-only section. Bryan will then contact you using the information in the membership roster to verify your identity.
  3. Once you are granted access, visit the members-only section at members.kc23.org

If you get the message "404. That's an error," then you likely have not yet been given access to the members site.

Feel free to contact Bryan at 407-796-1645 if you are having trouble accessing the site.
