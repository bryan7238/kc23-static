Guardian Angels Walkway Dedications

The Knights of Columbus, the Women's Guild, and St. John XXIII Parish are excited to offer 12x12 pavers, which will be placed on the walkway around the site for the St. Michael the Archangel statue. This is a wonderful way to pay tribute to a loved one who has served, or is currently serving in the military, as a first responder, as a doctor, or as a nurse.

The price for each paver is $350. To order a paver, please complete the ORDER FORM (PDF). Payments made by check help us to reduce our credit card processing fees. Payments may also be made online by credit card.

Pay by Check: Please make checks payable to Knights of Columbus. The price for each paver ordered is $350. Checks and the order forms may be delivered to a Knights of Columbus St. John XXIII officer or mailed to: Knights of Columbus, 13060 Palomino Lane, Fort Myers, FL 33912.

Pay by Credit Card: To pay by credit card, click the "Buy Now" below. This will take you to our secure PayPal payment portal, where you can pay using either a credit card or a PayPal account. You may send your order form by email to info@kc23.org
